Listing Template Tutorial

Thank you for choosing our template. If you have any questions or suggestions please feel free to contact us.

Using Template Generator

  1. Download the zip file.
  2. Unzip the files and double click on template-generator.html
  3. Enter username.
  4. Enter storename/username or the name you'd like to appear on the header of the template.
  5. Enter your product title, price, payment information, etc.

Adding Photos to the template generator

Adding photos to your listing, Please note that your photos must be hosted online. You may use the photos you have uploaded to eBay or third party image hosting service.


Using eBay Photos

  1. Go to your listing page.
  2. Click on the main product photo to enlarge.
  3. Right click on the photo and select "Copy Image Address"
  4. Open the template generator.
  5. Paste the URL on the space provided.
  6. Insert the photo tag to briefly describe your photo. It's not required, but strongly recommended.

Generate HTML Code

  • After you're done click "BOOYA" to generate the code.
  • Select all and copy the code.

    • Using the template on eBay

    • Log in to your eBay account.
    • Look for "My eBay" tab at the top of your screen.
    • Go to your Selling page.
    • Follow the steps to setting up your listing categories, title, etc.
    • Scroll down the the description box. You'll see two-tabbed editor. Click HTML tab and paste your listing template code here.
    • After you are done, you may click on Standard tab to to make final changes to the template.
